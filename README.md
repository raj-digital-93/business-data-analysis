📊 **Power BI Business Performance Dashboard**

📌**Overview**
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze overall business performance across multiple dimensions. It provides insights into sales trends, profitability, customer behavior and team performance to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 **Objectives**

Track key business KPIs such as revenue, profit, YOY% growth and target achievement
Analyze performance across product categories and customer segments
Monitor manager and team-level target achievements
Identify trends and patterns over time
Highlight key business risks and insights

📂 **Dashboard Pages**

**1. Executive Summary**

High-level overview of KPIs
Year-over-Year (YoY) performance analysis
Overall sales and profit trends

**2. Product Category Performance**

Revenue and profit by category
Identification of top and underperforming products
Profit margin analysis of products

**3. Customer Segment Performance**

Sales contribution by customer segments
Segment-wise profitability
Customer behavior insights

**4. Manager & Team Lead Performance**

Target vs achievement analysis
Performance comparison across managers
Identification of top and low performers

**5. Time-Based Analysis**

Monthly and yearly sales trends
Growth patterns and seasonality insights


📈 **Key Insights**

Strong Year-over-Year sales growth 
Target achievement is 99.6%
West Region contributed more sales and profit
Consistent decline in profit margins over time
Indication of volume-driven growth over profitability

🔍 **Detailed Insights**

🛍️ **Product Category Insights**

Identified top-performing categories contributing the highest revenue
Identified loss making categories with zero profit
Furniture category contributed more sales with less profit margin
88% products are profitable
Indication of a shift toward low-margin, high-sales products

👥 **Customer Segment Insights**

The Consumer segment contributes the highest profit margin, making it the most valuable customer group in terms of profitability.
Consumer segment has the largest share of customers and drives higher profitability.
Out of 804 total customers, 585 customers placed 5+ orders. This shows very strong repeat purchase behavior.

📅**Time-Based Insights**

Business moved from slow growth → high growth phase. 2024 shows strongest performance.
Profit margin is consistently declining after 2022 peak. Revenue is growing faster than profitability
Every product category shows year-on-year sales growth
Furniture shows consistent profit decline year-on-year

✅ **Recommendations:**

Shift focus from revenue growth to profitable growth
Reduce unnecessary discounts and introduce tiered pricing (basic, premium, luxury)
Promote more premium products and negotiate logistics cost
Introduce profitability KPIs alongside sales targets
Review pricing strategy for Furniture category
Introduce category-wise profitability dashboards for managers
Focus marketing on Consumer segment (high profitability driver) and introduce upselling / cross-selling strategies for other categories
Forecast future profitability, not just sales
